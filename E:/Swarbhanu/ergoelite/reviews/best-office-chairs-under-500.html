💡 What to Look For in a Budget Ergonomic Chair

Must-Have Features

  • Adjustable lumbar support — Height at minimum, depth if possible
  • Seat depth adjustment — Critical if you're under 5'8" or over 6'2"
  • Mesh back — Foam backs trap heat after 2+ hours
  • 3+ year warranty — Budget chairs with 1-year warranties are red flags

Nice-to-Have Features

  • 4D armrests — Height, width, depth, pivot
  • Headrest — Great for video calls and reclining
  • Synchro-tilt — Back and seat tilt together naturally
  • Carpet casters — Soft wheels for hard floors
