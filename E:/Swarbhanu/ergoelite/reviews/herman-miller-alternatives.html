Why Pay More for Herman Miller?

If these alternatives are so good, why do Herman Miller chairs cost over $1,500? There are three main reasons:

  • The 12-Year Warranty: Herman Miller offers a legendary, bumper-to-bumper 12-year warranty that covers everything, including labor. Most budget alternatives offer 1-5 year limited warranties.
  • Build Materials: Herman Miller uses incredibly durable, proprietary materials (like the Aeron's 8Z Pellicle mesh) that can withstand a decade of 24/7 use without sagging.
  • Resale Value: A 10-year-old Aeron can still sell for $400-$500 on the used market. Budget chairs typically have zero resale value after a few years.

The Verdict: If you view a chair as a 15-year investment and have the budget, Herman Miller is worth it. However, if you need relief from back pain today and only have $350, the alternatives on this list will provide 90% of the ergonomic benefits for 20% of the price.
