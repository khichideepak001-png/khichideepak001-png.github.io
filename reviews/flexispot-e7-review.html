Quick Verdict

The FlexiSpot E7 Pro is the best standing desk under $700, and it's not particularly close. It matches the Uplift V2 Commercial's 355 lb weight capacity, delivers excellent stability for a dual-monitor setup, and includes anti-collision protection — all for $600–$700 less than our top pick. The 5-year warranty is the main step-down, but for most home office users, that's a perfectly acceptable trade for $700 in savings.

The E7 Pro's 1.1° wobble at standing height is excellent — only the Uplift V2 Commercial (0.8°) beats it. For 95% of users, the stability is outstanding.
