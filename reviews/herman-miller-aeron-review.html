The PostureFit SL — What Actually Makes This Chair Special

Most ergonomic chairs support only the lumbar (lower spine). The Aeron's PostureFit SL supports two zones simultaneously:

🦴
Sacral Support (Lower)

The lower pad contacts the sacrum — the triangular bone at the base of your spine. This anchors your pelvis in a neutral, slightly forward-tilted position, which is the biomechanically correct seated posture.

🔄
Lumbar Support (Upper)

The upper pad supports the lumbar vertebrae, maintaining the natural inward curve of the lower back. Together with sacral support, this maintains the S-curve of your entire spine.

Our consulting physical therapist's assessment: "The PostureFit SL is the most anatomically correct production lumbar system I've encountered. Most chairs either support too high or too low. The dual-zone approach is how the spine actually works — it's not a gimmick."

Result in our testing: After switching from a $350 "ergonomic" gaming chair to the Aeron, test subjects reported an average 67% reduction in end-of-day lower back fatigue within 2 weeks.
